# First Text — What We Send the Moment a Form Comes In

**When:** The second a lead submits the signup form.
**Goal:** Confirm who they are, confirm what they want, end with a question. Get a response.

---

## Standard — Parent Enrolling a Child

```
Hi {parent_first_name}! 👋 This is {director_name}, {title} here at {location_name}!

I would love to get you all of our information to register with us! I see you are looking for {instrument} lessons for {student_first_name}! Is there a day or time that works best for you? 😊
```

---

## Standard — Adult Signing Up for Themselves

```
Hi {student_first_name}! 👋 This is {director_name}, {title} here at {location_name}!

I would love to get you all of our information to register with us! I see you are looking for {instrument} lessons! Is there a day or time that works best for you? 😊
```

---

## Variant — Adult, Has Experience or Specific Goals

```
Hi {student_first_name}! 👋 This is {director_name}, {title} here at {location_name}!

I would love to get you all of our information to register with us! I see you are looking for {instrument} lessons and {experience_detail}! Is there a day or time that works best for you? 😊
```

> Example `experience_detail`: "have some experience and are looking to advance" / "are a complete beginner" / "are looking for weekday mornings"

---

## Variant — Zach Voice (Casual Adult Lead)

```
Hey {student_first_name}! This is Zach, owner at {location_name}

Saw you were looking for some {instrument} lessons? 😀
```

> Use when the lead is an adult male, inquiry is brief/casual, or Zach is the assigned director.

---

## Variant — Multiple Instruments Selected

```
Hi {first_name}! 👋 This is {director_name}, {title} here at {location_name}!

I would love to get you all of our information to register with us! I see you are looking for {instrument_list} lessons! Is there a day or time that works best for you? 😊
```

---

## Variant — Lead Came From a Referral

```
Hi {first_name}! 👋 This is {director_name}, {title} here at {location_name}!

I would love to get you all of our information to register with us! I see you are looking for {instrument} lessons and were referred to us — may I ask who referred you? 😊
```

---

## Variant — Instrument Unknown (Rare)

```
Hi {first_name}! 👋 This is {director_name}, {title} here at {location_name}!

I would love to get you all of our information to register with us! What instrument are you looking for? 😊
```

> Only use if the form came in with no instrument selected.

---

## What Happens After

```
First text sent
  → They respond → go to 03-questions/ or 04-booking/
  → No response after 24h → go to 02-no-answer/
  → "Wrong number" / "who is this" → clarify identity, re-confirm
  → "Not interested" → go to 06-pushback/ (hard no path)
```

---

## Rules

- Always use their first name — never "Hi there" or "Hello"
- Always end with a question — never a closing statement
- Never mention price in the first text
- Never mention a specific teacher
- One emoji max per message
- If instrument is unknown, ask before anything else
