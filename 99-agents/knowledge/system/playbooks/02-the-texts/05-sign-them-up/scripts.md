# Sign Them Up — Closing and Confirming Enrollment

**When:** Lead said yes to a time slot, or they're clearly warm and ready.
**Goal:** Send the registration link immediately. No more questions. Close it.

---

## Send the Registration Link — The Moment They Say Yes

```
Please fill out our registration form to lock in your time! Welcome aboard! 😀
{registration_link}
```

> The second they agree — send this. Do not ask another question. The link is the close.

---

## With Slot Confirmation

```
You got it! Please fill out our registration form to lock in your {day} at {time} spot. Be sure to hit Submit when finished. Welcome aboard! 😀
{registration_link}
```

---

## Parent Enrolling a Child

```
Wonderful! Please fill out the registration form for {student_first_name} to lock in the {day} at {time} spot. Welcome to {location_name}! 😊
{registration_link}
```

---

## They're Warm But Haven't Said Yes Yet — Assume the Sale

```
Are you ready to get started? I can lock in your {day} at {time} right now — just fill out the form below! 😊
{registration_link}
```

> Use when everything is agreed — price is clear, slot is picked — but they haven't typed "yes." Assume the sale. Send the link.

---

## After the Form Comes In — Confirm It

```
Form received, thank you! We'll see you {start_date} at {time}. We'll be in touch before then. 👍
```

---

## Welcome to the Studio

```
Welcome to {location_name}! 🙂 We're so excited to have you. See you {start_date}!
```

Great news — if you register by {promo_deadline}, you get {promo_benefit}! Here's the form to lock in your spot:
{registration_link}
```

## Returning Student Coming Back

Welcome back! So glad to have you again. Here's the registration form to get you back on the schedule:
{registration_link}
```

---

## They Got the Link But Haven't Filled It Out Yet

**24 Hours Later:**
```
Hi {first_name}! Just wanted to check — did you get a chance to fill out the registration form? Here it is again: {registration_link} 😊
```

**48 Hours Later:**
```
Hi {first_name}! Just a quick reminder — your {day} at {time} spot is still reserved. Fill out the form to lock it in! {registration_link}
```

---

## What Happens After

```
Registration link sent
  → Form comes in → send form confirmation + welcome message
  → No form after 24h → send 24h reminder
  → No form after 48h → send 48h reminder with slot urgency
  → They say they're not ready → go to 06-pushback/ (need to think path)
```

---

## Rules

- Send the registration link the moment they agree — no delay, no extra questions
- Never ask "are you sure?" — don't create friction at the close
- Form confirmation must go out fast after form submission (automated)
- Never promise a specific teacher in the close message
- Welcome message is warm and brief — they're in, keep it simple
