# Booking — Getting Them to Pick a Time

**When:** Lead knows the price and is ready to pick a time.
**Goal:** Offer specific slots, get them to commit. Never give a menu — give a choice.

---

## Standard — Offer One Slot

```
I have a {day} at {time} available — would that work for you? 😊
```

> Always offer 1-2 specific slots. Never say "what works for you?" without giving an option first. Too many choices = delay.

---

## Slot With Urgency (Limited Availability)

```
We are filling up pretty fast, but I do have a {day} at {time} available right now. Would that work for you? 😊
```

> Use when their preferred day has limited openings.

---

## Two Options (Lead Hasn't Said Which Day They Prefer)

```
I have a {day1} at {time1} and a {day2} at {time2} available. Would either of those work? 😊
```

> Two options max. Any more than that and they stall.

---

## They Gave a Preferred Day — Lock It In

```
{day} works great! I have a {time} available — does that work for you? 😊
```

---

## Their Preferred Day Has No Openings

```
I don't have anything on {preferred_day} right now, but I do have a {alt_day} at {alt_time} available. Would that work? 😊
```

---

## Slot Is Filling Up — Create Urgency

```
Just a heads up — that {day} at {time} slot is going fast. Want me to hold it for you? Just fill out the registration form and it's yours! 😊
{registration_link}
```

---

## Our Hours (If They Ask)

```
Our hours are:
Mon–Thu: 3:30pm–9pm
Sat–Sun: 10am–3pm
Closed Fridays

We're filling up, so I'd love to lock in your spot! What day works best? 😊
```

---

## They Ask About the Teacher

```
All of our teachers are trained professionals — we'll match you with the best fit based on your goals and schedule. Once you're registered, I'll introduce you! 😊
```

> Never promise a specific teacher. Teachers change. Match on fit, not name.

---

## They Want to Come In First (Meet and Greet)

```
Absolutely — we love doing meet and greets! Is there a day this week that would work for you to stop by? We're open Mon–Thu 3:30–9pm and Sat–Sun 10am–3pm. 😊
```

---

## What Happens After

```
Slot offered
  → They say yes → go to 05-sign-them-up/
  → They want a different day → check availability, offer an alternate
  → They're not sure yet → "No worries! I'll hold this in mind. What day works best in general?"
  → They ask about the teacher → use teacher response above, pivot back to booking
  → They go quiet → go to 02-no-answer/
  → They ask for a meet and greet → use meet and greet response
```

---

## Rules

- Always offer a specific slot — never ask "what works?" without giving an option
- 1-2 options per message max
- Never mention teacher names in outbound booking messages
- If there's zero availability → escalate to human review, do not make up slots
- Always end with a question or a clear next step
