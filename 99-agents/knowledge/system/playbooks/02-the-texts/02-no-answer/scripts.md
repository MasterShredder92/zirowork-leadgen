# No Answer — What We Send When They Go Quiet

**When:** Lead hasn't responded to the first text (or any text).
**Goal:** Re-engage without pressure. Keep the door open. Never guilt-trip.

---

## Day 2 — 24 to 48 Hours After First Text, No Response

```
Hi {first_name}! Just wanted to check in and see if you had any questions about {instrument} lessons. We'd love to help! 😊
```

**Variant — If We Know Their Preferred Day Has Openings:**
```
Hi {first_name}! Just wanted to reach back out — we do have some great {preferred_day} availability right now. Would love to get you going! 😊
```

---

## Day 4 — 48 to 72 Hours After Day 2, Still Nothing

**Option A — Tell Them More About Us:**
```
Hi {first_name}! This is {director_name} from {location_name} again. Just wanted to share a little more about us — we've been open {years} years across 4 locations in the Omaha area. All of our teachers are trained professionals. Lessons are 1-on-1, 30 minutes, $160/month — no contracts. What day works best for you? 😊
```

**Option B — If There's an Active Promo:**
```
Hi {first_name}! {director_name} here from {location_name}. We're currently running a promotion — {promo_text}. Would love to get you started! What day works best? 😊
```

**Option C — Keep It Short (If Day 2 Already Had Details):**
```
Hi {first_name}, do you have any questions I can answer for you? 🙂
```

---

## Day 7 — Final Message, Last Attempt

```
Hi {first_name}! {director_name} here from {location_name}. Just wanted to reach out one more time — we'd love to be part of your music journey whenever the timing is right. Feel free to reach out anytime! 😊
```

> This is the last automated message. After this, mark the lead as **cold** and stop all outreach.

---

## Cold Lead — They Come Back After 30+ Days

```
Hi {first_name}! {director_name} here from {location_name} — so glad you reached back out! We'd love to get you going. Are you still looking for {instrument} lessons? 😊
```

---

## Seasonal Re-Engage — Proactive Outreach to Cold Leads

**Back to School (August):**
```
Hi {first_name}! Back to school season is here — a great time to add music lessons to the routine! We'd love to get {student_first_name} on the schedule. Interested? 😊
```

**New Year (January):**
```
Hi {first_name}! Happy New Year! If music lessons are on the list for {year}, we'd love to help. We're scheduling now — want to grab a spot? 😊
```

**Summer (May/June):**
```
Hi {first_name}! Summer is a great time for music lessons — more free time, more progress! We'd love to get {student_first_name} on the schedule. Interested? 😊
```

**General Seasonal (Any time):**
```
Hi {first_name}! This is {director_name} from {location_name}. I know we chatted a while back about {instrument} lessons — just wanted to reach out as we're scheduling for {season}. Would you still be interested? 😊
```

---

## What Happens After

```
Follow-up sent
  → They respond → pick back up from wherever they left off (pricing / booking / close)
  → No response after Day 7 → mark cold, done
  → "Not interested" → go to 06-pushback/ (hard no path)
  → "We signed up somewhere else" → "Congrats! We hope you love it. We're here if you ever need us! 😊"
```

---

## Rules

- Only 3 automated follow-ups total: Day 2, Day 4, Day 7
- **Never** say "I've tried to reach you several times" — banned
- **Never** mention how many times you've reached out
- Day 7 is always gentle — keep the door open, that's all
- If they respond at any point, stop the follow-up sequence immediately and resume the conversation
- Seasonal re-engage: one message per season maximum — if they don't respond, wait until next season
