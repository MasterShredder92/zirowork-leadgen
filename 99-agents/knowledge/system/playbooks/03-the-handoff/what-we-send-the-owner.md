# What We Send the Owner When Someone Signs Up

When a lead fills out the registration form and it comes in confirmed, the school owner gets a notification automatically.

---

## What the Owner Gets — The Summary

Here's the format of what gets sent:

```
New enrollment at {location_name}!

Student: {student_first_name} {student_last_name}, age {student_age}
Instrument: {instrument}
Day/Time: {booked_day} at {booked_time}
Experience: {experience}

Contact: {parent_first_name} {parent_last_name}
Phone: {phone}
Email: {email}
Military: {is_military}

Notes: {personality_notes}

How they found us: {referral_source}
```

---

## Example — Real Lead

```
New enrollment at Gretna!

Student: Jake Smith, age 9
Instrument: Guitar
Day/Time: Tuesday at 4:30pm
Experience: None

Contact: Sarah Smith
Phone: (402) 555-1234
Email: sarah.smith@gmail.com
Military: No

Notes: Beginner. Visual learner. Wants to learn his favorite songs.

How they found us: Facebook/Instagram
```

---

## How the Owner Gets It

**Two options — pick one (or both):**

**Option A — Text to owner's phone:**
The summary above gets sent as an SMS to the owner's number the moment the registration form is confirmed.

**Option B — Email:**
The summary gets emailed to the owner with the subject line: `New Enrollment — {student_first_name} {student_last_name} @ {location_name}`

> Zach — decide here which you want. Text is fastest. Email is easier to search later. Both is fine too.

---

## What the Owner Needs to Do After Getting It

1. Add the student to the school's scheduling system
2. Confirm the teacher assignment
3. Send the welcome text to the family (or let the agent do it — see 02-the-texts/05-sign-them-up/)

---

## What's Already Done by the Time the Owner Gets This

- Lead reached out via text
- Agent worked them through pricing and scheduling
- Lead picked a day and time
- Lead filled out the registration form
- Agent sent the "form received" confirmation and welcome message

The owner doesn't have to do any of the selling. They just receive the packaged new student.
